About Chambers Constructions

Established in 1995 by Managing Director Stephen Chambers, Chambers Constructions Pty Ltd provides high quality civil construction services throughout New South Wales, with our office located in the Clarence Valley. Our aim is to deliver the highest possible job satisfaction for our clients.

We offer various services to meet your project requirements. Whether you need assistance with pipe laying, civil and road services, sand, soil, and gravel supplies, or traffic control services and equipment, we've got you covered. Our fleet is well maintained and ready for hire, no matter how big or small your project needs. We are available 7 days a week, statewide, to ensure your projects run smoothly.

Interested in learning more about how Chambers Constructions can bring value to your next project? Don't hesitate to get in touch with us. Our friendly and knowledgeable team is eager to discuss your requirements.
